Daily spectrogram plot archive

These spectrograms are for one UT day. They have a frequency resolution
of 0.1 Hz, and a time resolution of 10 minutes. This shows tremor very well.
One plot is created for each volcano for the UT day. The data is archived
from the near-real-time spectrogram code.
